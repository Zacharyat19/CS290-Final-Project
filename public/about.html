About Us

Inspired by potato messaging services like Potato Parcel, as featured on the popular reality tv show Shark Tank, Potate strives to fill that void in digital potato messaging. Nowhere else on the internet will you find an experience quite like this! Simply write out a message, upload your favorite photo, and bada bing bada boom, you've got yourself your own personalized potato with which you can engage the digital potato community at large.
